I was selected to be lead designer for this client which had gone to RFP. The client wished to incorporate a "seaside" theme since the location for the event would take place seaside in San Diego, California.
I wanted to give the illusion of floating once attendees entered the main space by constructing the central hub with white fabric tensile structures.
Dividers between areas of the floor were sail-shaped tensile structures, giving the illusion of soft movement throughout the space.
Out of 4 advertising agencies across the nation, this design was selected as it best represented their vision for their 2020 event.
Sadly, because of unforeseen events, this event was cancelled. However, it still remains as one of my proudest accomplishments.
