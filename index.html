Hello, I'm Cat.

I am a frontend developer + designer with a background in architecture and event design with a deep love of music and coffee ☕️

Armed with 15+ years of experience designing physical spaces, I apply a systems-thinking approach to solving complex design problems. While designing corporate events for TechCrunch, Tableau, RedHat, and SaaStr, I gained exposure to innovative software products and was intrigued by the realm of opportunities available in software engineering. This exposure led me to exploring web design and eventually joining a full stack web development training course with Southern Methodist University where my interest in front end development blossomed.

I champion collaborating with engineers of all levels in the design process to create efficient + creative solutions and am eager to work with designers to create products that consumers love.

When I'm not working, I like to pick through antique and record shops, recreate recipes from instagram, try new restaurants, read historical non-fiction books, learn new things, and enjoy drinks on a cool patio with friends.
